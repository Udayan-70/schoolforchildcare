Udayan Chakma

Asst Teacher
TEWFA Ideal School

I started my teaching career on 2nd January 2000 AD by joining Babuchhara Mukh High School as an assistant teacher. After teaching there for eight long years, I joined Chittagong Metropolitan School and College in 2007 as an assistant teacher. After teaching there for a year, I joined the current workplace TEWFA Ideal School in 2008 and am still there. In addition to teaching, I successfully completed basic computer training at the Department of Youth Development in 2011. Then in 2013 I successfully completed the course on 'Content Base Management' organized by the Ministry of Education. Later on, I am in the final stage in HTML, CSS, JAVA, PHP, c ++ programming language from various online based programming learning sites and 'W3school.com'.
